Our Mission

At ReRoute, our mission is to empower formerly incarcerated individuals to reclaim their purpose and reenter the workforce with confidence. We provide tech training, resume tools, and direct job-matching support to help rebuild lives, reduce recidivism, and foster inclusive hiring.
